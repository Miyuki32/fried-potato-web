The Nintendo Family

The Nintendo Switch


  • OG Switch: This is the original model released in 2017.
  • Hybrid Powerhouse: Docks for stunning visuals on your TV and transforms into a portable console with detachable Joy-Con controllers.
  • Standard Battery Life: Provides a decent battery life for portable gaming, but not as extensive as the Switch V2.
  • Versatile Joy-Cons: Offer multiple configurations for different playstyles (attached to console, tabletop mode, or separate controllers).

Nintendo Switch Lite


  • The Portable Powerhouse: Designed specifically for handheld gaming.
  • Lightweight and Compact: Perfect for on-the-go adventures due to its smaller size and lighter weight compared to the standard Switch.
  • Non- detachable Joy-Cons: Integrated controllers offer a streamlined design but limit flexibility for docked play or tabletop mode.
  • Cost-Effective Option: A more affordable choice compared to other Switch models.

Nintendo Switch OLED


  • The Premium Pick: This upgraded version boasts several enhancements over the original Switch.
  • Enhanced Display: Features a vibrant 7-inch OLED screen for richer colors and improved contrast, especially noticeable in handheld mode.
  • Improved Battery Life: Offers slightly extended battery life compared to the original Switch, similar to the Switch V2.
  • Higher Price Point: Comes at a premium price compared to the other Switch models.
